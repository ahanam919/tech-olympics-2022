General Tips: Disposal

  • Make sure to refer to this app to find out if you’re disposing of the drug in the right place!
  • When disposing of medicines (those with warnings in particular), it’s recommended to mix some sort of foul tasting items with them, such as coffee grounds and cat litter. This applies when throwing medicines in the trash (not dropping it at a pharmacy)
  • Always be careful when handling liquids, small pills, and sharp objects, making sure that you don’t spill dangerous product, or injure yourself. Now, I know that I sound like your freshman biology teacher droning on about lab safety rules, but they both exist to keep you alive :)
  • When going to a pharmacy:
  • If you have a lot of medicine piled up that needs to be disposed, make sure to call ahead and ask if they have the capacity
  • Come with your pills and creams in their packaging and an external plastic bag
  • In general, call the center to make sure they take the kinds of medicine that you want to dispose of.
  • When you arrive, talk to a staff member, preferably a pharmacist, about where to go or who to talk to about disposing of the medication.
  • And, in these unprecedented times, wear your mask and stay safe!
